- Total Sales
- Gross Margin %
- Units Sold
- Stock Value
- Out-of-stock Risk SKUs
- Overstock Risk SKUs

- Sales trend by date
- Category contribution by revenue
- Top 10 products by gross margin

- Category and subcategory sales

- Overstock and stock-out flags

## Page 4: Replenishment Priorities

- Priority table by product/store
- Reorder now products
- Stock-out risk products
- Recommended action field

## Page 5: Data Quality Checks

- Missing product IDs
- Missing store IDs
- Duplicate sale IDs
- Negative stock checks
- Source-to-report reconciliation status
